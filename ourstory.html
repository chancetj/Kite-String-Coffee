The Kite String

Kite String Coffee was built on the concepts of connection and community. We believe a great cup of coffee is best enjoyed while connecting with someone else as a part of a community. As it is with a kite, one person holds the kite string while the kite dances in the sky. Sometimes you are the kite, and sometimes you are the one holding the kite string. The kite string makes this connection possible.

A Family Tradition

Kite String Coffee was established during the year of 1963 in Gregory Jacobson's tool shed. He invited his co-workers to come and try his coffee one day. The coffee that Gregory was making was so good that his co-workers kept coming back to get some of his coffee. It soon turned into an every morning thing to meet at Gregory's tool shed for a cup of joe before heading out to work. The surrounding community started hearing about Gregory's coffee and wanted to try it. While still operating out of a tool shed, he could not accomodate all the customers that were coming to him. Gregory started delivering coffee every morning to his customers so they could get their morning fuel without waiting too long at the tool shed. Since then, the buiness has stayed within in the Jacobson family, being passed down to sons and daughters. Kite String Coffee has expanded on the idea of delivering coffee and transitioned from delivering just fresh brewed coffee to whole bean and freshly ground coffee. Today, Kite String Coffee delivers all across the world.

Let us bring you coffee

Can't make it to one of our coffee shops? That's okay! Let us bring our coffee to you. Kite String Coffee delivers freshly ground and whole bean coffee made to order directly to your doorstep whenever you need it. Our variety of coffee is sure to form to any flavor palette.
